About

Find Out More About Us

SCIL is a reputed name in the industrial refrigeration industry around the world having served hundreds of companies in varied industries. The company is one of the few that deal with very low temperature system design and maintenance of process chillers, evaporative condensers, control panels, starter panels, heat exchangers and pressure vessels.
